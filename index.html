What is SIESGST's College Event Management System (CEMS)?

"In all forseeable circumstances, components of general features of the interactive services detection provides a strict control over The Emergency of Insufficient Idea" (Murray Acker in The Book of the Outline Design Stage).

Create your own Event!

"In all forseeable circumstances, components of general features of the interactive services detection provides a strict control over The Emergency of Insufficient Idea" (Murray Acker in The Book of the Outline Design Stage).

See all events you have participated in!

You will get to see about all the events, in which you have participated. In this way, you won't forget in which event you have particpated.

Monitor your spendings/earnings and count!

You will get to see the cost of all the events and also the amount you have given. So you can thus keep a check on your spendings.

Explore from a wide range of GST-made events!

You can see all the events that are being conducted on this website and thus you can decide whether to take part or not based on your interests.

Check easily to see if an event is Active or Not

If under any technical circumstances, the event gets cancelled or it is on hold, then you will get to know about it through this website

Follow your teams!

You will able to see who your group memebers are and with whom you have participated in.

Send request to participate

If you are interested in some event then you can send request to the organiser to participate in that event.
